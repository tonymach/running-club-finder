How It Works

Welcome to the Running Club Finder! Here’s how you can find a running club near you:

  1. Select your province or region from the dropdown.
  2. Browse through the list of available running clubs in your area.
  3. Click on a club to see more details, including running days, times, and the club’s social links.
  4. If you can’t find your city or a club near you, you can recommend a new club or start one yourself!
